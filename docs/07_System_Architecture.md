# CryptoTrade OS — System Architecture

## Mission

CryptoTrade OS is not just a trading journal.

It is a complete operating system for traders that combines:

- Trading Journal
- Portfolio Management
- AI Coach
- Analytics
- Risk Management
- Trading Psychology
- Daily Planning
- Performance Tracking

The goal is to become the main application that a trader opens every day.

---

# Core Modules

## 1. Dashboard

Purpose:
Daily overview of trading activity.

Contains:

- Capital
- Today's PnL
- Open Positions
- Recent Trades
- Daily Checklist
- AI Coach Summary

---

## 2. Trading Journal

Purpose:

Store every trade with complete information.

Features:

- Add Trade
- Edit Trade
- Delete Trade
- Screenshots
- Notes
- Emotions
- Mistakes
- Strategy

---

## 3. Portfolio

Purpose:

Track investment portfolio.

Supports:

- Spot
- Futures
- Stocks
- ETFs
- Crypto Wallets

---

## 4. Analytics

Purpose:

Generate professional trading statistics.

Examples:

- Win Rate
- Profit Factor
- Expectancy
- Average RR
- Average Win
- Average Loss
- Drawdown
- Monthly Statistics

---

## 5. AI Coach

Purpose:

Personal AI trading mentor.

Responsibilities:

- Analyze trades
- Detect mistakes
- Detect emotions
- Suggest improvements
- Build weekly reports

---

## 6. Daily Planner

Purpose:

Help trader prepare before trading.

Contains:

- Daily goals
- Market checklist
- Economic calendar
- Personal notes

---

## 7. Psychology

Purpose:

Improve trading discipline.

Contains:

- Mood tracking
- Confidence score
- Fear / Greed level
- Revenge trading detector

---

## 8. Settings

Contains:

- Profile
- Exchanges
- API Keys
- Notifications
- Theme
- Language
